All Our Services

01

Annual Boiler Service

A full safety and efficiency check to keep your boiler running reliably all year. Includes a Gas Safe certificate on completion.

02

Boiler Repair

Fast diagnosis and repair across all major brands. We give you an upfront quote before starting — no nasty surprises on the bill.

03

Breakdown Response

Boiler stopped working? We offer same-day or next-day response to breakdowns across South Manchester and surrounding areas.

New Installation

Replacing an old, inefficient or unreliable boiler. We advise on the right unit for your home, supply it, fit it and commission it properly. Contact for quote.

Low or high boiler pressure? We find the root cause — whether it's a leak, a faulty PRV, or an expansion vessel issue — and fix it properly.
