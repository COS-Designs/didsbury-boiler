How It Started

Built on trust, not sales targets

Robin and Dom started Didsbury Boiler Servicing & Repair out of frustration. As experienced gas engineers, they'd seen too many customers overcharged, misled, or left waiting days for a simple fix. They knew they could do it differently.

Based in Didsbury, they set out to build a local boiler company rooted in honesty — transparent pricing before any work begins, no call-out fee surprises, and a commitment to getting it right first time.

Since launching, they've built a reputation across South Manchester for being the engineers you can actually trust. Every five-star review reflects the same thing: straightforward people doing quality work.

What We Stand For

Our principles, not just our pitch

01

Radical Transparency

You'll always know the price before we start. No surprises, no extras added on at the end. We quote honestly and stick to it — even if the job turns out to be more complex than expected.

02

First-Visit Fix Rate

We carry a comprehensive stock of the most common boiler parts on every visit. Our goal is to diagnose and fix your boiler in a single visit — not drag it out over multiple call-outs.

03

Local, Independent

We're not a franchise. We're not a call centre. We're two local engineers who live and work in South Manchester. When you call us, you speak to us — and we show up ourselves.

04

Same-Day Response

Boiler problems don't keep business hours. We aim to respond to all calls the same day, and offer emergency breakdown response for those situations that simply can't wait.

05

Fully Insured & Gas Safe

All our work is covered by full public liability insurance. We're Gas Safe registered — which isn't just a badge, it's a legal requirement. You should never let an unregistered engineer near your gas appliances.

06

Honest Advice, Always

If your boiler doesn't need replacing, we'll tell you. If a repair isn't worth the cost, we'll say so. We'd rather give you the right advice than make a sale we'll regret.

GAS SAFE
Credentials & Registration

Legally registered. Fully covered.

Gas Safe registration isn't optional — it's the law. Anyone working on your gas appliances must be Gas Safe registered. We're registered under RK Gas Safe, which means every job we do is legally compliant and fully insured.

You can verify our registration at any time on the official Gas Safe Register website. We'll always carry our ID card on every visit — ask to see it.
